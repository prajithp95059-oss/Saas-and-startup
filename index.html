How is Stacky different from stitching together point tools?

Every module — analytics, automation, billing and workspace — shares the same underlying customer record, so nothing needs to sync through Zapier or a nightly export. Actions in one module trigger the others instantly.

Will this replace my existing spreadsheet workflows?

Most teams migrate their core metrics in under a week using our guided importer. You can run Stacky alongside spreadsheets during the transition, then retire them once your team trusts the dashboards.

What does the free trial include?

Full access to all modules for 14 days, no credit card required. You can invite your whole team and connect live data from day one.

Do you support custom roles and permissions?

Yes — founders, ops, support and engineering each get tailored dashboards, and you can define custom roles with granular module-level permissions on the Growth plan and above.

Is there a limit on team seats?

No hard limits on any plan. Pricing scales with usage and active workspace members, and we'll always flag it before you're billed for growth.
